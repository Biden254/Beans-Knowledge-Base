# Relationships Between Beans, Pests, and Pesticides

## Pest → Bean Relationships
- Aphids infest Common Beans
- Aphids infest French Beans
- Whiteflies infest French Beans
- Bean Weevils infest Kidney Beans
- Spider Mites infest Common Beans
- Thrips infest Lima Beans

## Pesticide → Pest Control Relationships
- Neem Oil controls Aphids
- Pyrethrin controls Whiteflies
- BT controls Cutworms
- Insecticidal Soap controls Spider Mites
- Diatomaceous Earth controls Bean Weevils

## Pesticide Safety Relationships
- Neem Oil is safe for all bean types
- Permethrin is not safe for Lima Beans
